01 — About

The mind behind the work

I started in marketing and spent 5 years across B2B and B2C, covering content, campaigns, branding, and growth. Somewhere along the way, I realised the part I enjoyed most was understanding how the product worked and why people chose it.

A website revamp project changed everything. Taking something old and abandoned and watching it come alive again for real users gave me a sense of fulfilment I hadn't felt before. That's when I understood what drives me: building things that actually matter to the people using them.

I'm actively seeking roles in Product Marketing or Product Management, looking to bring a marketer's instinct and an AI-enabled mindset to a product team.
